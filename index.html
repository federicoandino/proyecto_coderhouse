Who is Casey Neistat? Casey Neistat lives New York City and says he 'loves' Youtube. He is an American filmmaker, vlogger, and co-founder of the social media company Beme.
Neistat and his brother, Van, are the creators of the HBO series The Neistat Brothers which first aired in 2008.
Prior to moving to New York City, dad-of-two Neistat worked as a dishwasher at a restaurant.
His first job in New York City was as a bike messenger. In 2001 he began making films with his brother Van about the artist Tom Sachs.
The 35-year-old never went to film school or college but now boasts 6,067,480 subscribers on Youtube and more than a billion hits.
He joined YouTube in Ferbruary 2010 and has gone on to amass nearly one million followers on Twitter.
Neistat’s first big break came in 2003 when he created a viral video about Apple’s battery problems. Five years later, he sold an autobiographical TV series called The Neistat Brothers to HBO for $2 million.
Since then, he’s been making videos on YouTube charting his adventures and experiences that have been viewed more than 200 million times. He’s also moved into app development, launching a product called Beme that aims to make video sharing more real by preventing people from seeing or editing the videos they take before sharing them.
